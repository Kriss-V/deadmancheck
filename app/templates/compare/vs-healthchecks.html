The difference that matters

Healthchecks.io monitors one thing: did your job ping in time? If it didn't, you get an alert. That's the dead man's switch model, and it works well.

But there's a second failure mode nobody talks about: the job starts, but never finishes. A database backup that hung at 98%. A sync job stuck on a locked record. A scraper crawling 10× slower than expected. These all ping on schedule — so Healthchecks.io stays green while your infrastructure quietly falls behind.

DeadManCheck tracks both. When a job takes longer than its rolling average (or your hard limit), you get alerted — even if it technically started on time.
